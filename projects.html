All members/users of the site can view the ads, save ads as favorites to revisit later. This website does not provide messaging mechanism between members to kick off the purchase/rent. Python

The goal of this exercise is to build a web application (named Careermate) that provides a platform for users to post their interview experience/Company review online. Other users can benefit from these reviews. Other users can answer/respond to these postings. For example, a user could post his interview experience at XYZ Company listing the questions that were asked at the interview and the overall process. Other users can respond with their answers to the interview questions so other users can benefit too. Any platform that provides users the freedom to post is much better off with some moderation. So there are admin users too, who respond to reports from other users about spam/irrelevant/offensive content on the website.

Site developed as part of coursework for COSC 630 by Akshatha Raj (araj0@frostburg.edu)
